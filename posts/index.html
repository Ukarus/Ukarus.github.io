Games

Games i have participated on, some of them just by myself and others in a team. I included the itch.io link in case you want to give it a try, most of the games can be played in the browser and they have their installer in case you want a better experience.

Games

The Grass is always Greener on the other Side

This is a simulation game about competing for a contest for the most beautiful garden, in which you must vandalize your neighbours garden’s and repair your house, while also avoiding cameras and dogs when attacking. It was made in collaboration with David094 and GrafEisenn and it was a submission for the Github Game Off 2022. Visit the Itch.io page.
Games

Ufo Invaders

Ufo invaders was a game i made for the CS50’s Introduction to Game Development course and it’s heavily inspired by Space Invaders. This game was my first attempt at Unity and it was a bit of a challenge at that time. It features a score mechanic, powerups, an infinite scrolling background and a score mechanic. Visit the Itch.io page!
Games

Cowboy 50

Cowboy 50 is a game i made as a final project for the CS50 course, it’s a very basic shoot’em up that includes powerups, lots of enemies, sounds, music and a very basic gameplay. It was made with Love2D and with some assets from itch.io, it’s a small clone of the mini game “Journey of the Prairie King” which can be played in Stardew Valley. Visit the Itch.io page!
Games

Boxes n' Barrels

Boxes and barrels was a game made in collaboration with kameronbsmiley, HumdrumParrot, ncollie and Ukarus (me). It was a submission for the Godot Wild Jam No 47. This is a puzzle game with a third person camera, it’s heavily inspired by the Sokoban games. Visit the Itch.io page!
Games

Rage and Snow

Rage and snow was the first game i did outside of a tutorial and the first one i made with a team, so it has a special place in my heart despite all it’s bugs and flaws. It was made for the “Open Jam 2018” and the theme for that jam was “Spam to win” so it’s a mix of platformer with a snowball spam mechanic. Visit the Itch.io website!
